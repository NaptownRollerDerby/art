  • infoI want to use your logo on my ad/website/etc. What's the process?

    1. Download the logo you wish to use from the main page of art.naptownrollergirls.com (this site).

    2. Design your item as you usually would

    3. When you're finished, collect completed sketches/comps/etc and put them on a publically accessible place on the internet (Dropbox, Google Drive, Amazon S3, your website, etc).

    4. Copy the link to your file(s) and submit a request.

    5. One of the creative team will take a look at your item and get in touch with you. (Usually, we're pretty quick, but we do get busy, so please allow some additional time for us to look over and discuss your item if necessary. Including a deadline on the form you fill out helps us out quite a bit.)

    6. We'll get back to you with an approval or recommendation on how you can utilize our logo in an approved manner.

  • assignmentI'm designing an ad for my business for a bout program. What are the specifications?

    Full page - 5.5" w x 8.5" h
    psd | ai | indd

    Half page - 5.5" w x 4.25" h
    psd | ai | indd

    Quarter page - 2.75" w x 4.25" h
    psd | ai | indd

    Please build your ad with a .25" bleed on all sides. Raster formats should be at least 300dpi and in .tif or .psd format. Vector formats should have all fonts outlines and saved as an .ai, .eps or .pdf file. All files will be converted to CMYK.

    Templates are available directly below the size listing to the left.

    Email your file to your NRG contact or directly to ads@naptownrollergirls.com.
