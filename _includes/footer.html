Usage Guidelines

Use of logos on this page fall under usage guidelines set by the organization. If you produce an advertisement, video, apparel item or anything else that includes one of these logos, you are required to submit an image of your design for creative approval. Please use the form at the top of the page to submit a request for consideration. Do not remove registered trademark symbols from any logo that you download.

Approvals for items using the NRG logo will be accepted via the submission form. Please allow us a reasonable amount of time to review your request and include any due dates to make sure we respond promptly.


NRG Creative

The Naptown Roller Girls creative team is made up of volunteers, who try to make cool stuff for the league and respond to requests in a timely manner. If you need assistance, please use the contact link or email creative@naptownrollergirls.com.

If you need us to create something for you or need a logo in a different size/format, please use the link at the top of the page to submit an official request.
